Farmstead Meats is an Australian direct-to-consumer meat supplier specialising in 100% grass-fed and grass-finished beef, pasture-raised lamb, and free-range chicken and pork. The company sources exclusively from small, ethical Australian family farms that prioritise animal welfare, natural grazing, and sustainable farming practices.

Farmstead Meats delivers premium bulk meat packs directly to households across Australia, offering hormone-free, grain-free, and minimally processed meat. All cuts are expertly butchered, vacuum-sealed for freshness, and prepared for convenient home freezer storage.

The brand focuses on ethical sourcing, nose-to-tail utilisation, and transparent farming standards — providing families with clean, natural protein raised as nature intended.

## Product Philosophy

Farmstead Meats specialises in bulk meat packs and whole-body purchasing options (1/8, 1/4, 1/2, and full body beef and lamb) to provide better value per kilogram while supporting whole-animal utilisation and reducing waste.

Products are designed for:
- Families seeking freezer-ready meat
- Health-conscious consumers
- Buyers prioritising hormone-free protein
- Customers wanting ethical, paddock-to-plate sourcing

## Nutritional Positioning

Grass-fed and pasture-raised meats are naturally rich in protein, iron, zinc, B vitamins, and beneficial fatty acids. Farmstead Meats emphasises clean, minimally processed whole-food protein sourced from animals raised on natural diets.

## Key FAQs

Q: Is your beef 100% grass-fed and grass-finished?
A: Yes. All Farmstead Meats beef is raised entirely on pasture and never grain-finished.

Q: Do you deliver Australia-wide?
A: Yes. We deliver across Australia using insulated cold-chain packaging.

Q: Is your meat hormone-free?
A: Yes. Our livestock is raised without added growth hormones.
