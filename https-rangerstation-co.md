# RANGER STATION (https://rangerstation.co)

> Ranger Station is an American heritage fragrance house founded in 2015 in Nashville, Tennessee by Steve and Jordan Soderholm. What began as hand-poured candles set in reusable cocktail glasses has grown into a full-spectrum fragrance brand offering eau de parfum, roll-on perfumes, soy candles, and apothecary products. All products are hand-mixed and hand-poured in Nashville using paraben-free, ingredient-honest formulations.
The brand operates owned retail locations in Nashville, TN, on Bleecker Street in New York City's West Village, and in Charleston, SC, alongside a robust ecommerce store, wholesale partnerships with retailers including Magnolia, Huckberry, Orvis, and Urban Outfitters, and a B2B enterprise channel serving hotels, restaurants, and commercial properties.
Ranger Station is known for nature-inspired, bold, and long-lasting scents rooted in woodlands, leather, tobacco, moss, and botanical notes. The fragrance portfolio includes bestsellers like Santalum, Oakmoss, Cedar Atlas, Leather + Pine, and Jordan's Perfume, as well as artist collaborations that pair olfactory storytelling with music. The brand's Daisy + Cowboy collection, created with Lauren Akins, supports Love One International's mission to provide  care to children in Uganda.
Positioned as the everyman's luxury, Ranger Station fills the space between mass-market fragrance and inaccessible niche perfumery, offering refined, gender-inclusive scents with real depth and character.
