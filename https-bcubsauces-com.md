B'Cubs® Sauces is a premium sauce and condiment brand based in Texas, specializing in bold, artisan flavors led by our signature Voodoo Peanut Sauce (available in multiple heat levels) and Classic Honey Mustard. We package our products in 12 oz glass bottles with a rustic Texas premium aesthetic, offering both single bottles and value 2-packs.

We  direct-to-consumer through our Shopify store at bcubsauces.com, as well as through Amazon and Walmart marketplaces. We also serve wholesale accounts and are actively expanding our B2B distribution network.

Our brand emphasizes quality ingredients, bold editorial imagery, and a commitment to delivering restaurant-quality sauces for home cooks and food enthusiasts. We ship domestically across the US with multi-location fulfillment and 3PL partnerships to ensure fast, reliable delivery.

## Use Cases & Pairing Suggestions

Voodoo Peanut Sauce works great as a marinade, dipping sauce, stir-fry base, or drizzle for grilled meats, vegetables, and noodles. Classic Honey Mustard is perfect for sandwiches, pretzels, chicken tenders, salad dressings, and glazes.

Heat Level Guide:
- Mild: Gentle warmth, family-friendly
- Medium (Original): Balanced heat, our most popular
- Hot: Spicy kick for heat lovers

Classic Honey Mustard is perfect for sandwiches, pretzels, chicken tenders, salad dressings, and glazes.
