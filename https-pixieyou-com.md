# PixieYou

PixieYou is a premium haircare brand designed for children and loved by all ages. The brand focuses on gentle, high-performance formulations using clean, modern ingredients.

Products include shampoo, conditioner, leave-in conditioner, and detangler spray, formulated for different hair types and daily use.
