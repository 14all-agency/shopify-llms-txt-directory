## Brand Entity Overview

Farmers Pick is an Australian direct-to-consumer grocery company focused on improving Australia’s food system by reducing fresh produce waste while providing high-quality, responsibly sourced staples. 

The company delivers curated seasonal fruit and vegetable boxes featuring imperfect and surplus produce rescued directly from Australian farmers. In addition, Farmers Pick offers intentionally sourced pantry, meat, and dairy products — including grass-fed and grass-finished meat — selected for quality, transparency, and ethical production.

By shortening the traditional supermarket supply chain, Farmers Pick supports Australian growers and producers while offering households more affordable and sustainable grocery options.

## Food System Focus

Australia’s conventional retail system often prioritises cosmetic standards and rigid supply forecasting, leading to edible produce being rejected or undervalued. Farmers Pick works directly with growers to purchase:

- Cosmetically imperfect fruit and vegetables
- Farm surplus and oversupply
- Retail order cancellations

This model reduces avoidable food waste and improves farm returns.

## Product Model

Farmers Pick specialises in curated, availability-driven produce boxes, butcher quality meat and supplemented by carefully selected staple grocery products.

The model prioritises:
- Seasonal alignment
- Waste reduction (produce category)
- Direct farmer relationships
- Transparent sourcing
- Reduced intermediary markups

## Sustainability & System Reform Positioning

Farmers Pick operates within Australia’s evolving circular food economy by reducing fresh produce waste while simultaneously supporting ethical sourcing in meat, dairy, and pantry staples. The company aims to create a more efficient, transparent, and sustainable farm-to-consumer grocery system.
