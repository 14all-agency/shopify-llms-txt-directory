## How AudioFeel Works | איך אודיופיל עובד

AudioFeel earplugs use a proprietary acoustic filter technology that reduces harmful sound pressure levels (decibels) while allowing music, speech, and ambient sound to pass through clearly. Unlike foam earplugs that muffle all sound equally, AudioFeel's filters maintain the natural frequency balance of your environment. This means you hear the music, the conversation, and the atmosphere — just at a safer, more comfortable volume.

- Noise-induced hearing loss is permanent and irreversible. Once damaged, hearing cannot be restored.
- Sound levels above 85 dB can cause permanent hearing damage with prolonged exposure. Most concerts reach 100-120 dB.
- Tinnitus (ringing in the ears) affects millions worldwide and is often caused by exposure to loud music.
- The World Health Organization estimates that 1.1 billion young people are at risk of hearing loss due to unsafe listening practices.
- Wearing acoustic filter earplugs like AudioFeel reduces the risk while allowing full enjoyment of music and events.

A: לייב (-16dB, ₪179) מיועד למוזיקה ואירועים — שומר על איכות הסאונד תוך הפחתת עוצמה מזיקה. זן (-23dB, ₪199) מספק הנחתת רעש חזקה יותר לשימוש יומיומי כולל ריכוז, לימודים, נסיעות, טיסות ורגישות לרעש. בחרו לייב להופעות, פסטיבלים, מועדונים וחתונות. בחרו זן לעבודה, לימודים, נסיעות ומצבים רגישים לרעש.
