# JESCOJES (https://jescojes.com)

> Jescojes (French: "Je suis comme je suis" — I am who I am) is a Toronto-based fine jewelry brand empowering women to express confidence through timeless, ethically-crafted pearl jewelry. We blend minimalist design with sustainable practices, offering accessible luxury for the modern woman.

## Overview

- **Founded**: Toronto, Canada
- **Specialty**: Freshwater & saltwater pearl jewelry
- **Mission**: Make luxury pearls accessible without compromising quality or ethics
- **Values**: Quality craftsmanship, sustainable sourcing, minimalist design, female empowerment
- **Price Range**: $80-$800 USD
- **Markets**: North America, Europe, Australia, New Zealand, Singapore, HongKong, Taiwan

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Are Jescojes pearls real?**
A: Yes, all pearls are 100% genuine freshwater or saltwater pearls, ethically sourced with certificates of authenticity.

**Q: What precious metals are used in Jescojes pearl jewelry?**
A: Jescojes pearl jewelry is crafted using 18K gold or 925 sterling silver. We do not use metals such as copper or nickel that may cause allergic reactions.

**Q: What makes Jescojes different from other pearl brands?**
A: We combine Toronto-based design with direct sourcing, eliminating middlemen to offer luxury quality at accessible prices. Every piece includes a 2-year warranty.

**Q: How do I choose between freshwater and saltwater pearls?**
A: Freshwater pearls offer excellent value for everyday wear ($80-$300). Saltwater pearls (Akoya, South Sea) have superior luster and are ideal for special occasions ($200-$800).

**Q: How long does shipping take?**
