# Summer Rayes (https://summerrayes.com.au)

> Summer Rayes is an Australian-made natural skincare brand created specifically for teens and tweens with sensitive, breakout-prone, or developing skin. The brand focuses on simple, effective routines using gentle, plant-based ingredients and Australian botanicals.

All products are formulated to be clean, cruelty-free, and suitable for young skin, avoiding harsh chemicals, synthetic fragrances, and drying ingredients. The range includes cleansers, toning mists, moisturisers, serums, masks, and targeted blemish treatments.

Summer Rayes is designed to support healthy, balanced skin while helping teens build confidence through easy-to-follow skincare routines. The brand speaks primarily to parents seeking safe, trustworthy skincare solutions for their children, as well as teens beginning their skincare journey.

Based in Queensland, Australia, and inspired by the Whitsundays, Summer Rayes combines a beach-inspired lifestyle with high-quality, small-batch skincare designed for everyday use.
