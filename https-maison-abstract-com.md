# Maison Abstract (https://maison-abstract.com)

> Maison Abstract is an online art store that offers curated wall art designed to enhance modern homes and office interiors. The site focuses primarily on abstract and contemporary styles, featuring a range of prints and canvas artworks inspired by well-known artists, mid-century European abstraction, and modern creative movements. Its collections include works influenced by artists such as Wassily Kandinsky and Piet Mondrian, as well as contemporary creators and themed series that highlight expressive colour, geometry, and layered composition.

The platform allows customers to browse different artist collections, explore framed canvas options, and select pieces suited to various interior design aesthetics. Maison Abstract promotes the idea of turning everyday spaces into personal galleries, offering artwork that reflects individual taste while bringing visual impact to a room. Overall, the website positions itself as a destination for stylish, gallery-inspired wall art that blends classic abstract influences with contemporary decorative design.
